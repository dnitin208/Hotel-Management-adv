About us

For more than five decades, Our Hotel has created countless memories for their customers highlighting the real meaning of hospitality.Our Hotel was started in 1963 by Mr.B.Babu Rao, Mr. B. Sundar Rao and Anand Rao. The hotel is strategically located in the heart of bustling city “Hyderabad”. Hotel elegantly furnished hotel with one and two-bedroom suites bring you a high level of comfort and sophistication that will make you feel at home; refinely designed to meet all the ultimate comfort needs. Relax in our cozy yet comfortable rooms with perks. that includes 34 well-furnished air-conditioned and non air conditioned rooms, LED TV’s, room service. All the rooms are equipped with free High-speed Internet access, hot and cold showers, in-room dining, and much more. Complimentary amenities include buffet breakfast and ample parking, and there are also restaurants that serve multi cuisine vegetarian Indian food from morning 6 am to 10:30 pm. We have also extended services that include undertaking outdoor catering. Our has specially designated indoor spaces for hosting conferences, weddings, seminars, parties, events and other social functions.
